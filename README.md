# OSM Preprint 2026

LaTeX preprint manuscript analyzing open data sharing trends across ~326,000 biomedical research articles from PubMed (2024-2026).

**Key Findings:**
- Major funders, journals, and institutions demonstrate dramatically higher rates of open data sharing (as high as 82%) compared to the baseline rate of 13.5% for recent open access research articles
- Analysis of top repositories identified in data sharing statements reveals patterns in repository adoption across research domains

**Interactive Dashboard:** https://www.opensciencemetrics.org
